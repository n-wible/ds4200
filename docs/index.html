Nick's Photography Site

I’m Nick, a Data Science & Finance student at Northeastern. When I'm not studying or working, I usually relax by either working on my car, or spending time in nature by hiking and doing photography. I shoot on an older Sony A6000, with a Sigma 16mm f/1.4, or the 16-50mm and 55-210mm kit lenses. I only really like taking landscape shots, and focus a lot on nature-related shots. Though I have a great camera, the majority of my shots are from my iPhone 14 Pro, because I can't be hauling a camera everywhere.

Editing the photo is often more important than the original photo itself, and I use Adobe Lightroom because its free from the University. I also love to travel, and the photos on my website are from a LA and Bos respectively. In the past, I've lived and worked in South Florida (born & raised), Atlanta, Boston, and the Bay Area. I've also had the opportunity to visit a few different areas, like upstate NY, NYC, upstate NH, LA/SoCal. A few special parks I've visited also include the Salar de Uyuni, Grand Canyon, Muir Woods, Everglades/Big Cypress, and the Mojave. Soon I hope to have the chance to visit Acadia.
